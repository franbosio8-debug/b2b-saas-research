## Post 2 (June 2026)  
For over a year, JUV Consulting has been working with Coach to build Coachtopia—today, this project was announced to the world. Coachtopia is Coach’s first sub-brand in its 80+ years, and it is a brand committed to a circular fashion ecosystem. It is a brand that sells products that are built from waste and designed to be remade and reused. 

It is also a brand that emblematizes the work we do at JUV because of its commitment to community. We talk a lot about the “community-first” mindset at JUV, about “building with” not “building for,” and this is what that approach to business looks like in the real world. Coachtopia is a brand backed by 100+ Gen Z creatives, creators, and changemakers who JUV has helped recruit to be a part of the Coachtopia community over the last year. They have consulted/created/tested/critiqued and been involved in conversations at the highest level. I have seen members design graphics that make it to production, model the clothes shown to consumers, strategize the digital marketing behind launch, and be featured in the campaign. And we have done this all while building a community of cool, diverse young people along the way. 

In a marketing/advertising world that has decided that “authenticity” is the most valuable thing—this is what authenticity looks like. Not just including those you’re trying to reach in the conversation, but letting them lead it. 

This is a brand that will resonate with Gen Z, because it empowered Gen Z to build it.
