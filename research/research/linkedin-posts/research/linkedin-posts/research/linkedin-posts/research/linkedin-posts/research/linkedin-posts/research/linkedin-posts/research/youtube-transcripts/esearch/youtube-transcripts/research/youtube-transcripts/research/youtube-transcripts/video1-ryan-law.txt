## Video: "How AI is Changing SEO Strategy"

**Key insights from Ryan Law (CXL):**

- "AI is transforming SEO from a keyword game to an audience understanding game"
- "The best use of AI is for content ideation and research, not writing final copy"
- "SEO professionals need to become better editors and strategists"
- "Human oversight is critical for accuracy and brand voice"
- "AI tools help scale what works, but strategy still comes from human insight"

**Main takeaways:**
- Use AI to analyze search intent at scale
- Human writers create authentic content that connects with readers
- The future belongs to those who can blend AI efficiency with human creativity
